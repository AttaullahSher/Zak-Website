About Us

ZAK Music (Zak Electronics & Musical Instruments Co) has been at the heart of the UAE’s music and electronics scene for over four decades. We are dedicated to bringing the joy of music and technology to everyone — from passionate beginners to seasoned professionals. Our commitment is simple: to offer the finest instruments, electronics, expert guidance, and services that inspire creativity.

Whether you need an instrument, electronics, a repair, a rental, or a professional studio, ZAK Music is here to make your musical and technological journey seamless and unforgettable.
